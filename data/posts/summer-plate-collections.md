# Summer Plate Collections

Each Sunday, half of our undesignated offering — the "plate" — goes to a community partner doing justice work in our area. This summer we are proud to support the following organizations.

## This Summer's Partners

- **Local food security** programs serving families across MetroWest
- **Immigrant and refugee** support networks
- **Environmental justice** initiatives in our watershed

When you give during summer worship, you extend the reach of our congregation's values into the wider world.

Thank you for your generosity.
