# Summer Services Update

Summer is here, and with it our cherished season of lay-led worship. From late June through early September, we gather in the **chapel** for a more intimate, reflective service.

## Schedule

- **Time:** Sundays at 10:30am
- **Place:** The Chapel (enter through the main doors)
- **Format:** Lay-led, with music, readings, and shared reflection

Several services this summer will be offered jointly with neighboring congregations — watch the weekly email for locations.

## Stay Connected

If you can't join us in person, our **Fellowship Hour** continues on Zoom after each service. Whether you're traveling or staying close to home, you remain part of this community.

We look forward to worshiping with you.
