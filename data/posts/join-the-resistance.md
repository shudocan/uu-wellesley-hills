# Join the Resistance

Our faith calls us to *just and responsible action*. This season, there are many ways to put our values into practice alongside fellow members.

## Where to Plug In

1. **Black Lives Matter Vigil** — Join our monthly standing vigil in front of the meetinghouse.
2. **Climate Action** — Help advance our congregation's environmental commitments.
3. **Advocacy** — Connect with state and local campaigns for justice and human dignity.

No experience is necessary — only a willingness to show up. Reach out to our Social Justice Team to learn more.

Together, love calls us forward.
