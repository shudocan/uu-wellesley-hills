About the Camp

Our Summer Kids Camp is a fun and engaging program designed for children of all ages. Each week is packed with exciting activities, crafts, outdoor adventures, and opportunities to make lasting friendships. Led by experienced and caring staff, we aim to create a safe and inspiring environment for every camper.

Join us this summer and let your kids experience the joy of community, creativity, and discovery!

Schedule & Registration

The camp runs weekly throughout summer, Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 3 PM. Early drop-off and late pick-up options are available for an additional fee.

Spaces are limited, so sign up today to reserve your child's spot!
