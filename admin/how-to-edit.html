Editing the Church Website

A quick guide. You only ever use the Website Editor — you never need to know anything technical.

Every time you want to make a change

  1. Open the editor: go to uuwellesleyhills.org/admin (bookmark it!). It remembers you, so you can start right away.
  2. On the left, click the page or list you want to change (Home, Events, Blog posts, and so on).
  3. Edit the boxes — type new text, or click Upload image to swap a picture.
  4. Click 👁 Preview to open a new tab and see exactly how it will look.
  5. Happy with it? Click Publish. You'll see “✅ Published — live in about a minute.” That's it!

If you make a mistake

Don't worry — nothing is permanent. Scroll to Recent changes at the bottom and click Undo last change. The website goes right back to how it was.

Good to know

  • Events & Announcements are lists — use + Add for a new one, Remove to take one down, and the ↑ ↓ arrows to reorder.
  • Blog posts live under “Blog posts.” Click + New post, write your text, and Publish.
  • You can change the colors/theme visitors see from the website itself (the ✦ button) — but that's just a preview for you. The site remembers each visitor's own choice.
  • Changes take about a minute to appear on the live site. If you don't see it right away, wait a moment and refresh.

Need help?

Email the office or reach out to Andrew. When in doubt, use Preview before you Publish.
